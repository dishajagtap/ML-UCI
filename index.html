Who We Are

Artificial Intelligence at the University of California, Irvine (AI@UCI) is a nonprofit student-run organization that focuses on promoting and cultivating the discipline of machine learning and its applications among the UCI community. So far, machine learning has shown the most promise of developing and applying artificial intelligence concepts in real-world practical approaches. We as a club try to take advantage of machine learning’s current and futuristic potential.

What We Do

  • Supervised Learning

    A machine learning-oriented procedure that analyzes labeled training data and produces an inferred function so that when new inputs are included, the procedure has a higher chance of predicting the correct output.

  • Reinforcement Learning

    A subfield of machine learning inspired by behavorial psychology, concerned with how software agents ought to take action in an environment to maximize a reward.

  • Transfer Learning

    A subfield of machine learning that specializes on storing knowledge gained while solving one problem and applying it to a different but related problem.
